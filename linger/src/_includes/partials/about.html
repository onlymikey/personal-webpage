About me

Hi. I'm Mikey.

I'm a computer engineering student from Mexico. I spend most of my time on Linux, messing with systems, and building things from scratch. I don't really follow trends or whatever is supposed to be "the right way" to build things, if something interests me, I just dive into it and figure it out as I go.

Most of my projects start as small ideas or experiments and slowly turn into something bigger. Sometimes they work, sometimes they don't, but that's not really the point.

I spend a lot of time listening to music, playing games, and going down random internet rabbit holes that somehow always turn into new ideas. I don't really like what the modern web has become, so this site is my way of doing things differently.

Nothing here is really finished, and that's kind of the point.
