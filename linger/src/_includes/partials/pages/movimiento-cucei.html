web movimiento CUCEI

Website for the student movement at CUCEI, built to share information and keep things organized and accessible.

context

this project came from a real need inside CUCEI

information was scattered, communication was inconsistent, and there wasn't a clear or reliable space where things could be understood or followed

what started as "we need a website to explain what's going on" quickly turned into something else

the problem wasn't just the lack of a website, it was the lack of structure around how information was shared and organized

this became an attempt to build a space that could give clarity, context, and continuity to something that was already happening in real life

what it offers

  • a centralized place for information, updates, and documents
  • a clearer way to understand what's happening and why
  • a structure that makes things easier to follow and navigate
  • a space designed around accessibility and context, not noise

process

this project was less about building something complex and more about figuring out how to organize things in a way that actually made sense

a lot of the work went into restructuring everything, removing redundant sections, deciding what mattered, and making sure the flow was clear

it also meant rethinking decisions that looked good visually but didn't actually help people understand anything

at some point, it stopped being just a website and became something closer to a system that evolves with the movement itself
